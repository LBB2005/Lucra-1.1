## The Problem We're Solving

### The Research Gap

Retail investors have three options for stock research today:

1. **Free tools** (Robinhood, Webull, Yahoo Finance) — surface-level data, no analysis, no synthesis
2. **Subscription content** (Seeking Alpha, Motley Fool, newsletters) — one person's opinion, often behind paywalls, not interactive
3. **Professional platforms** (Bloomberg Terminal: $27,000/year, FactSet: $12,000+/year) — institutional-grade but completely inaccessible to individuals

Nothing in between does *real analysis* — the kind where you get a DCF model, insider transaction data, macro context, technical signals, and sentiment all synthesised together. That work either takes hours of manual effort or access to tools that cost more than most people's entire investment accounts.

### Why Now

Large language models crossed a quality threshold in 2024–2025 that makes multi-agent financial analysis genuinely useful. The combination of models like Claude Sonnet with real-time data APIs (SEC EDGAR, Finnhub, Perplexity) means you can now run the equivalent of a full analyst team on any stock in minutes. This wasn't possible two years ago.

## Tone of Voice

**Confident, not arrogant.** Lucra is making a strong claim ("hedge fund-quality research") and needs to back it up with specificity — cite real methodologies, real data sources, real numbers.

**Sophisticated, not jargon-heavy.** The target user is financially literate but not a Bloomberg power user. Use terms like "DCF" and "insider transactions" without over-explaining them, but don't assume CFA-level knowledge.

**Precise, not fluffy.** Avoid vague AI marketing language ("unlock insights," "harness the power of AI"). Instead: "15 agents. SEC EDGAR data. Real-time Perplexity search. In 2 minutes."

**Honest about what it is.** Lucra is a research tool, not a financial advisor. The tone should reflect that — it helps you make better decisions, it doesn't make decisions for you.

---

## What Lucra Is Not

- **Not financial advice.** Every output is research to inform your decisions, not a directive to trade.
- **Not a black box.** Every agent shows its reasoning, sources, and methodology. You can see exactly why Lucra reached a conclusion.
- **Not a replacement for judgment.** Lucra makes you a better investor; it doesn't invest for you.
